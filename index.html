Know Your Impact

FoodFluence


We're creating a web application to help people understand the impact of their food choices
in a meaningful way. Our web application will allow users to choose from a list of foods
provided and learn about the food, its environmental impact, and how can users can make
alternative choices to decrease their impact. The goal of this web application is to encourage
users to make more environmentally conscious food choices by presenting alternatives to their choices.
